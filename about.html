About Me

My name is Márk Molnár, and I am currently in my third year of studying Applied Data Science & Artificial Intelligence at Breda University of Applied Sciences in the Netherlands.

I have a deep passion for solving complex data challenges using machine learning and AI. Analyzing patterns, building predictive models, and uncovering insights that drive meaningful decisions is something I truly enjoy. Through various projects, I have gained valuable hands-on experience that has further strengthened my enthusiasm for data science.

Programming excites me because it offers a world of clarity and logic, where every challenge has a solution. Seeing my efforts translate into real results is incredibly rewarding. I firmly believe that AI and IT are shaping the future, and I am eager to contribute to solving the evolving challenges in this dynamic field.

Beyond academics, I believe in maintaining a well-balanced lifestyle. Playing football and going to the gym helps me stay active and sharp, both mentally and physically.

As a lifelong learner, I am always looking for opportunities to expand my knowledge and skills—whether through personal projects, internships, or collaborations. I am known for my persistence and strong belief in self-improvement, driven by the idea that hard work and dedication make anything possible.

My Story

I was born and raised in Hungary, spending my early years in a small village where I attended elementary school. Later, I pursued my high school education at Varga Katalin High School in Szolnok, specializing in IT and Mathematics. It was during this time that I was introduced to programming, robotics, and linear algebra, which sparked my interest in technology and data science.

In 2023, I moved to the Netherlands to pursue my studies at Breda University of Applied Sciences. This was a significant step in my personal and professional growth, and living abroad has enriched my experiences, both academically and socially.

Outside of tech, I am a huge football enthusiast (once dreamed of going pro, but lets just say my skills didnt keep up). I also love traveling, exploring new cultures, cities, and cuisines, and I have a deep interest in architecture and tourism.

My journey has been filled with challenges and opportunities, and I am grateful for the support of my family, friends, and mentors who have helped me along the way. I am excited about the future and look forward to the adventures that lie ahead.
